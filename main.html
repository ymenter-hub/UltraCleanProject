About Us

At Ultra Clean, we're more than just a cleaning service; we're a team of dedicated professionals committed to creating spotless, healthy environments for our clients. Founded in 2015 by Sarah Johnson, our company was born out of a passion for cleanliness and a desire to provide exceptional service. From our humble beginnings with a small team, we've grown into a trusted name in the cleaning industry, serving both residential and commercial clients across the region.

Our team is the heart of Ultra Clean. Each member is carefully selected, rigorously trained, and fully insured to ensure the highest standards of quality and safety. We believe in fostering a positive and supportive work environment, which translates into the exceptional service our clients receive. We're not just employees; we're a family, united by our commitment to excellence and our shared goal of exceeding client expectations.

At Ultra Clean, we're driven by a set of core values that guide everything we do. We believe in integrity, transparency, and open communication. We're committed to using eco-friendly cleaning products and sustainable practices to minimize our environmental impact. Our ultimate goal is to build lasting relationships with our clients, based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service. We're not just cleaning spaces; we're enhancing lives, one spotless environment at a time.
